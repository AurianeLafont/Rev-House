Today we present you an unique venue on the French Riviera : Rev House.

Berthed at the Vauban harbor you will enjoy the glamorous, relaxing and breathtaking atmosphere of this unique venue. We can bring the Rev House to the harbor of your choice on the French Riviera.

The Rev House is an adaptable, flexible and moveable event space.

According to your needs and wishes we organize together with you your tailor-made event : a private lunch or dinner, a meetings, a wedding or any other kind of event.

Create the magic and a unique moment thanks to our new luxury Floating Penthouse.

We stay at your entire disposal to create your tailor-made event.

The interiour of the Rev House has been designed with sobriety and elegance to suit all your events. The living space is extremely versatile

Benefit from a fully-equipped kitchen, and a direct access to two bathrooms... The Rev House offers every convenience.

For intimate gatherings or business meetings, the spacious living area can be easily transformed.

With a fully-equipped kitchen, you have the freedom to bring in a personal chef or catering service to create delectable culinary experiences for your guests.

As you step onto The Floating Penthouse, you'll be greeted by a team of dedicated event coordinators who will work closely with you to tailor every detail according to your preferences.

The Rev House can be fully decorated according to your corporate identity, needs and wishes.

The most important part of this unique venue : The Rooftop. Enjoy it's wonderful 360° view. Your coffee break and lunch will suprise your guests.

Create together with us your personalized decoration, set up and atmosphere according to your brand image and surprise your colleagues, clients and guests.
